### Description — 4000 chars

```
English4Kids is a calm, ad-free English adventure for children ages 6 to 12. Built by educators and engineered for privacy, it turns short, daily sessions into confident first words, sentences, and stories.

WHAT KIDS DO

Children meet Milo the Fox and Luna the Owl, two friendly mascots who guide them through three units of activities:

• Listen & Tap — match the spoken word to the right picture
• Speak It! — pronounce a word and watch the green ring fill in
• Story Time — follow a short illustrated story with karaoke-style word highlighting
• Sing Along — sing original songs with synchronised lyrics
• Word Garden — earn seeds, sprouts, buds, blooms, and stars as words are mastered

Activities are short by design. Most lessons finish in five to seven minutes. There are no streaks, no leaderboards, no pop-ups, and no nudges to come back.

WHAT PARENTS SEE

A dedicated Parent Dashboard (locked behind a math gate so children cannot wander in) shows:

• Daily and weekly progress, by unit and by lesson
• The full word garden — every word the child has practised
• Recent lesson history with timestamps
• Full data control — export everything as JSON, schedule deletion with a 7-day grace window, or pause cloud sync at any time

PRIVACY POSTURE

• Cloud sync is opt-in only. The app works fully on-device by default. Parents who want progress on multiple devices complete an email verification flow (two confirmations 24 hours apart) before any data leaves the device.
• Microphone audio stays on the device. Pronunciation scoring runs through the operating system's on-device speech recogniser. Audio frames are never written to disk and never transmitted. Only a numeric score (0–100) can ever cross the network.
• No ads. No third-party tracking. No behavioural profiling. No advertising identifiers.
• Analytics are parent-only. The Parent Dashboard uses cookieless, EU-hosted analytics. Child-facing pages ship zero analytics scripts.

AGE BANDS AND CEFR

• Ages 6–8: early reader band. Bigger type, more pictures, shorter sentences, slower audio.
• Ages 9–12: independent reader band. Longer stories, faster songs, richer vocabulary.
• CEFR alignment: Pre-A1 entry, A1 by end of Unit 3.

COMPLIANCE

• COPPA (US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) — fully compliant.
• GDPR-K (EU General Data Protection Regulation Article 8) — fully compliant.
• UK Age-Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code) — all 15 standards addressed.

WHAT IS NOT IN THE APP

• No ads.
• No chat or messaging with other users.
• No leaderboards or social pressure mechanics.
• No in-app purchases in this release.
• No third-party login.
• No video uploads, no photo uploads, no contacts access.

WHAT IS COMING

• More units, more songs, more stories.
• Additional languages for the interface (currently English and Turkish).
• A teacher mode for classroom use.

We are a small team of teachers and engineers. Write to us at hello@english4kids.app — we read every message.
```
