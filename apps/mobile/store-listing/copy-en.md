## Full description (Apple ≤ 4000 / Play ≤ 4000)

```
English4Kids is a bilingual English learning app for children ages 6 to 11. Built by educators, designed for families, and engineered for privacy from the ground up.

KEY FEATURES

• Story-driven vocabulary. Every word your child learns lives inside a short story they can read, listen to, and act out with friendly mascots Milo and Luna.
• Pronunciation practice. Your child speaks a target word and gets gentle, on-device feedback. Audio is processed entirely on the device — it never leaves the phone or tablet.
• Spaced review. The app revisits tricky words at just the right time, using a Leitner-box system trusted by language teachers for decades.
• Two ages, one app. Activities adapt to age band 6-8 (early readers) or 9-11 (independent readers).
• Bilingual support. Switch between English and Turkish (more languages coming) so your child reads instructions in the language they're most comfortable with.

PRIVACY FIRST

• No ads. Ever.
• No third-party tracking. We don't even know your child's name unless you type it in (and even then, it stays on the device).
• No data collection. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal information.
• Designed for families. Compliant with COPPA (US), GDPR-K (EU), and the UK Children's Code.

GREAT FOR

• Bilingual families wanting their children to keep up English at home.
• Schools and tutors looking for a homework companion that respects student privacy.
• Parents of early ESL learners who want story-led, screen-time-friendly practice.

WHAT'S DIFFERENT

Unlike apps that show your child cartoons in exchange for data, English4Kids works fully offline once installed and keeps every microphone sample on the device. You'll see exactly what your child is doing — no surprises, no hidden purchases, no upsells inside the kid view.

We're a small team of teachers and engineers. Email us at hello@english4kids.app — we read every message.
```
